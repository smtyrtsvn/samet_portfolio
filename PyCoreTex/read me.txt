📦 Textile ERP & Advanced Stock Management System
A comprehensive, cost-oriented inventory and financial management application specifically designed for textile businesses. This system streamlines the transition from raw production costs to international sales by integrating Landed Cost calculations, EAN-13 barcoding, and Current Account (Cari) tracking into a single modern interface.

🚀 Core Features
1. Precision Costing (Landed Cost Engine)
FOB-based Calculation: Automatically calculates total costs by adding freight, local transport, and lab testing fees to the factory price.

Automated Expenses: Integrated logic for VAT (21%), Customs Brokerage (0.5%), and Bank Fees (0.5%).

Dynamic Currency Parity: Real-time conversion from USD to EUR based on user-defined parity rates, allowing for accurate European market pricing.

2. Intelligent Inventory & Barcoding
Automated EAN-13 Generation: Every unique SKU (Color/Size variant) automatically receives a mathematically valid, unique EAN-13 barcode upon creation.

Smart Stock Tracking: Separate reporting for "Stock Status" (Quantities) and "Financial Status" (Values), featuring automated calculation of In-flows and Out-flows.

3. Financial & Current Account Management (CRM/SRM)
Ledger System: Full tracking of Supplier debts and Customer credits.

Transaction History: Record manual payments and collections to maintain an accurate real-time balance for every business partner.

Profitability Analysis: Detailed SKU-level reporting on Total Cost vs. Total Sales to monitor Net Profit/Loss.

4. Professional Documentation & Integration
Master-Detail Invoicing: Advanced UI to list past invoices and examine itemized details with a single click.

PDF Export: Generate professional Proforma Invoices in PDF format.

Excel Integration: Support for bulk product uploads (Import) and comprehensive reporting (Export) for Stocks, Finance, and Accounts.

🛠 Tech Stack
Language: Python 3.10+

UI Framework: CustomTkinter (Modern, Dark Mode compatible interface)

Database: SQLite3 (Relational data management)

Data Processing: Pandas (Excel engine and complex reporting)

PDF Engine: ReportLab (Dynamic document generation)

Excel Handling: OpenPyXL
